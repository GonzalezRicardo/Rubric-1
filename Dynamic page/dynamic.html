Breif story of my life

I was born in a small town in Mexico called Charcas, located west of San Luis Potosi, Mexico. A very small area where everybody knew each other. I cant talk much about my experience living there because I was so little when I left. My parents reason for leaving was to give my older brother and I, a better future. They left everything they had for us. At that time, I was a baby. We moved to the states when I was two and a half years old, along with my big brother who was 3. Ever since then, I have lived in the same area not to far from where I live now.
